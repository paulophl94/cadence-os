Morning Briefing

Begin your day with full context — no more 30 minutes of reconstruction.

  • Calendar and meeting prep loaded
  • Initiative and project status pulled
  • Stale commitments flagged (>3 days without update)
  • Today's Three priorities suggested (max 3 P1)
  • Session context restored from yesterday

Documents, Reviews & Meetings

Generate and refine documents. Process meetings as they happen.

  • Create PRDs, One-Pagers, specs with full context
  • Run multi-perspective reviews (6 personas)
  • Process meeting notes → auto-update person pages
  • Track commitments with unique IDs
  • Use frameworks for strategic decisions

Daily Review

Reflect on what happened. Capture learnings and prep tomorrow.

  • Compare planned vs actual (Today's Three)
  • Capture learnings and preferences
  • Career evidence prompt for significant work
  • Session context saved for tomorrow
  • Stale tasks and commitments flagged

Weekly Review

Synthesize the week. Set up next week for success.

  • Week synthesis with patterns identified
  • Quarter progress evaluation
  • Top 3 priorities for next week suggested
  • Toolkit improvements surfaced from backlog
  • Unused skill recommendation (feature discovery)
